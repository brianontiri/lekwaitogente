About


Lekwaito Gente is an Event Management company that is comprised of passionately creative multi-disciplinary talented team. We are passionate and innovative in our art of connecting Brands & People. Our approach that is diversified is at the core of our creativity and inspiration.

In our Maze ooh we have fun. We mongrelize ideas, innovate cultures to sustain the ever-changing industries, and laugh. When you engage our services, the result will be nothing short of a creative, insight driven brand rejuvenated experience that will be flawlessly executed and truly connect with your target audience. Founded in 2018 we aspire to be a globally recognized and award-winning full-service event management and Marketing agency. Our growth and success will be directly attributed to not just our client services, creativity, strategy and execution; but also, our clients. The believe that only true partnership, that are mutually rewarding can drive sustainable long-term growth.
